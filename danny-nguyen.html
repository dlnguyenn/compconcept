About Me:

My name is Dan. I am a junior. I am studying Information Technology. + I am a part of Nu Alpha Phi fraternity, which is an Asian-interest social fraternity here at Rutgers. Currently, I spend most of my time either working on school, my career, or my fraternity. I love to talk to others. I also love reading. One of the craziest things I did this semester was put my tuition refund into stocks and lose all of it due to a market crash.

At first, I was getting lots of money. My return was 80%.

However, when I woke up one day, all of it was gone.

Well, in reality, half of it was gone.

Thinking that if I waited the price would go back up again, I waited.

It only went back down.

It did not feel real.

However, although now I am not rich in wealth,

I am rich in knowledge.
