About JC:

Be Like Water.


Be like water, Bruce Lee once said.
I heard it from his daughter,
And now Bruce Lee is dead.

I try to understand what it means.
It means to flow,
But I have a mind of a bean.
I dont really know.

I understand water is gentle and strong.
But that sounds like a contradiction.
Maybe I should write a song,
And sing about my addiction.

I dont get it,
Im losing my mind.
I need the answer, I must admit.
I know its worth the find.

I wish I could ask Bruce Lee myself,
So I can be like water.
I wanna move on and put this on a shelf.
I need to know the meaning from the author.

To be like water, I guess its out of reach.
Ive let it go, maybe Ill be like air.
Ive stopped seeking, then it came to me at the beach.
To know the answer, water would not care.
